Collection Books - A Photo & Memo Journal

Record and manage plants, animals, and other hobbies with photos and notes! This is your personal guidebook app for all your collections.

About "Collection Books"

"Collection Books" is an app that lets you record and manage all your cherished collections and discoveries, turning them into your very own original guidebook. No complex operations or specialized knowledge are required. Anyone can easily, conveniently, and freely give shape to their passions.

From daily plant observations and hobby collections to travel memories and pet growth logs... easily record your passions with photos, images, and notes. This is your personal digital encyclopedia! It's a handy journaling tool to organize and manage your valuable discoveries and data, creating your very own treasure space.

What you can do with Collection Books

1. Save Photos & Register Memos

  • Name (Nickname, Scientific Name, etc.): Freely set titles to match your collections.
  • Photo Registration: Record your valuable collections with photos. You can register multiple images to capture records from various angles.
  • Memo (Free Description): Freely write down your impressions, detailed information, or personal thoughts.
  • Canned Text: Register frequently used phrases or information as canned text in the settings. Easily paste them with a single button when creating a page, allowing for efficient record-keeping.
  • Tagging Function: Manage pages by categorizing them. Set tags like "Category," "Location," or "Color" to suit your collection and organize it neatly.

2. Showcase Your Style! Abundant Display Templates

  • Basic Templates: Choose from various display methods, including simple guidebook-style templates.
  • Future Additions: We will continually add more convenient templates, taking user requests into account.

3. Instant Access to Information! Flexible Display & Search

  • Sorting Function: You can sort your collections by selected conditions such as name or creation date.
  • Keyword Search: Easily access the information you want by searching with keywords, such as specific names, memo content, or tags.

4. Protect Your Valuable Data! Secure Data Management

  • Backup & Restore Function: Easily back up your data and restore it when needed. Feel secure during device changes or in case of accidental data loss.

Why Choose Collection Books?

  • Easy to Use & Flexible: No complex operations required. Anyone can start recording easily, and customize display and entry fields freely.
  • Personalized Records: Specialized in individual records. You can wholeheartedly record your private "likes."
  • Visually Enjoyable: With photo registration and customization features, we've made recording itself a fun process.

Premium Features (Paid Content)

The free version is fully functional, but we also offer paid content for those who want to further enhance their Collection Books.

  • Page Image Output: Each created collection page can be exported as an image (PDF, JPG).
  • Batch Output of High-Quality Pages: Export recorded pages in high quality in a batch. This allows for printing or beautiful sharing of your collections.
  • Collection Data Management: Import and export data for each collection book.
