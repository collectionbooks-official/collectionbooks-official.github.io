Privacy Policy

This application (hereinafter referred to as "this app") recognizes the protection of user (hereinafter referred to as "user") personal information as a top priority and strives for appropriate handling based on the following privacy policy (hereinafter referred to as "this policy").

1. Information Acquired

This app may acquire the following information.

  • Personal Information:

    This app does not directly collect personal information such as name, address, phone number, gender, or date of birth. However, if you use the "Feedback/Requests" feature and send an email, your sender email address will be disclosed to us. Any personal information (name, other contact information, etc.) voluntarily entered by you in the email body is also included. When responding to inquiries, this app automatically includes information about the app version, OS version, and device model in the email body. This information is used solely for the purpose of responding to customer inquiries.

  • Non-Personal Information / Anonymized Information:

    This app may automatically collect information that cannot identify specific individuals (e.g., app usage status (frequency of feature use, usage time, etc.), crash information (date and time of occurrence, OS version, etc.), device type (model name, OS version, language settings, etc.)) for the purpose of improving services and fixing bugs. This information is aggregated as statistical data and used in an anonymized form that cannot identify specific individuals.

2. Method of Information Acquisition

  • Personal Information: Your sender email address and information contained in the email body are acquired only when you voluntarily send an email through the "Feedback/Requests" feature. Device information mentioned in "1. Information Acquired" is automatically included in the email body when composing the email.
  • Non-Personal Information / Anonymized Information: Automatically collected when the app is launched, when specific features are used, or when a crash occurs. This information may be recorded within the app to track usage or included in automatically generated system reports.

3. Purpose of Use of Acquired Information

  • Personal Information:

    For replying to inquiries, opinions, and requests from customers, and for necessary communication in connection therewith. We will not use your personal information for any purpose other than those stated above.

  • Non-Personal Information / Anonymized Information:

    For improving app features, developing new features, and enhancing quality. For identifying, analyzing, and fixing bugs in this app. For analyzing usage status and creating statistical data. For delivering advertisements displayed within the app.

4. Locally Stored Data

In this app, collection book data (photos, text, settings, etc.) created and entered by users are stored in the user's device's local database (Room) and private storage.

This data is not transmitted to the app provider's server and is managed solely within the user's device.

If the backup function is used, the data will be saved in ZIP format in a directory specified by the user within the device, according to the user's selection. This data is also not transmitted to the provider's server.

5. Provision to Third Parties

This app will not provide any information, including personal information, to third parties, except as required by law. Non-personal and anonymized information will also only be used in a form that does not identify individuals as statistical data, and will not be provided to third parties in principle.

6. Use of Information Collection Modules

This app may use the following information collection modules. These modules collect non-personal information for the purpose of providing this app, but do not collect information that identifies individuals.

  • Google Play Billing Library:
    • Provider: Google LLC
    • Purpose: Providing in-app purchase functionality and managing purchase history.
    • Collected Information: Purchase-related information (purchase ID, item ID, etc.), usage data. This information is used for providing and managing in-app purchase functionality, and user payment information (credit card information, etc.) is not disclosed to the developer.
    • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy
  • Ad Delivery Module (e.g., Google AdMob):
    • Provider: Google LLC
    • Purpose: Displaying advertisements within the app.
    • Collected Information: Device identifiers (advertising ID, etc.), IP address, app usage (ad display count, etc.), location information (with user permission), inferred interests, etc. This information is used for delivering personalized advertisements to users and for measuring ad effectiveness.
    • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

      *Users can limit ad personalization from device settings.

7. User Information Management

Personal information (e.g., email address) collected by this app is used solely for replying to your feedback and requests. If you request disclosure, correction, suspension of use, or deletion of information, we will promptly respond within a reasonable scope after verifying your identity.

Furthermore, settings related to ad personalization can be changed from your device's privacy settings (e.g., "Google settings" or "Ads").

8. Compliance with Laws and Cooperation

This app complies with the Personal Information Protection Law and other related laws and guidelines.

If disclosure is requested by public institutions such as courts or police, information may be provided based on law.

9. Security Measures

This app implements appropriate and reasonable security measures to prevent leakage, loss, destruction, or alteration of acquired information.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

This policy may be revised as necessary. If revised, we will promptly notify users within this app or on the official website.

11. Termination of Service Provision

If the provision of this app ends, or if the user uninstalls this app, data stored on the user's device (e.g., encyclopedia data) will generally be deleted. Users are responsible for backing up important data themselves.
